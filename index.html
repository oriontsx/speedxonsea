// capabilities

Everything a drop needs, in one chat.

01

Allowlist & public mints

Pick a collection, pick your mode. SpeedX verifies eligibility per wallet, handles auth and funds, and queues the mint — built around OpenSea SeaDrop.

02

Scheduled fires

Set the open time and walk away. The mint goes out on the block you asked for — not whenever you remember to hit the button.

03

Floor sweeps

Buy the cheapest N from any collection in a single command, with a price ceiling you set. Works on every supported chain.

04

Copytrade

Point it at a wallet. When they buy, you buy — the same item, in real time, across the networks you've armed.

05

Multi-wallet

Generate fresh wallets or import your own. Fund them, run them together, and sweep everything back to one address when you're done.

06

Listings & offers

List your items or accept the top offer across every wallet at once — no per-wallet babysitting.

// questions

Before you ask.

Which networks does it support?

Every network OpenSea supports — Ethereum, Base, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Zora, Blast, Avalanche, Sei and more. As OpenSea adds chains, SpeedX follows.

Do you hold my keys or funds?

No. SpeedX is non-custodial. You generate or import your own keys and you control every wallet. Nothing is held on your behalf.

How do I get access?

Access is gated. Message @speedxonseabot to pair — it's approved, not an open free-for-all.

What does it cost?

You pay the mint price and network gas, same as minting yourself. SpeedX automates the speed and the multi-wallet logistics around it.

Is this risk-free?

No. Minting and trading carry real financial risk, and you're responsible for your own keys and transactions. Use what you can afford to lose.
