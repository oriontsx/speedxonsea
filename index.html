// capabilities

Everything a drop needs, in one chat.

01

Allowlist & public mints

Pick a collection, pick your mode. SpeedX verifies eligibility per wallet, handles auth, funds, and queues the mint. Built around OpenSea SeaDrop.

02

Scheduled fires

Set the open time and walk away. The mint goes out on the block you asked for, not whenever you remember.

03

Floor sweeps

Buy the cheapest N from any collection in one command, with a price ceiling you set.

04

Copytrade

Point it at a wallet. When they buy, you buy — the same item, in real time.

05

Multi-wallet

Generate fresh wallets or import your own. Fund them, run them together, sweep everything back to one address when you're done.

06

Listings & offers

List your items or accept the top offer across every wallet at once.

// three steps

From chat to minted.

  1. 01

    Get in

  2. 02

  3. 03

    Run it

    Choose the drop, the mode, and the time. SpeedX does the rest and reports back.
